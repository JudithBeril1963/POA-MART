Who we are

Run by students, for students

Nafuu started with a simple observation: the small campus stalls that freshers rely on in their first week charge more than they need to for the same padlocks, notebooks and bathroom basics. We stock the same essentials and pass on the difference, while still running a fair, sustainable business.

We know first week is when trust matters most, which is why we don't ask for any payment upfront. You only pay once your order is physically in your hands and you've checked it's correct.

  • Prices checked against the campus market for every item we list
  • No markup on true essentials like noodles: priced to match, not to profit
  • Order confirmed by a real person on WhatsApp, not an automated system
  • Delivery straight to your hall or hostel, no trip to the stalls required
  • Zero upfront payment: nothing changes hands until your order is in front of you

Nafuu is an independent, student-run supply service serving the University of Nairobi community. It is not an official University of Nairobi enterprise.

Questions answered

Frequently asked questions

Do I have to pay anything before delivery?

No. We never ask for a deposit or upfront transfer. You confirm your order on WhatsApp, we deliver it to your hall, and you only pay (cash or M-Pesa) once you've checked it's correct.

How is this cheaper than the campus market?

We check our prices against what campus stalls charge for the same item and price just above cost, rather than adding a full retail markup. Essentials like noodles are priced to match exactly. We're not trying to profit on the basics.

Do you deliver to all halls and hostels?

Message us on WhatsApp with your hall or hostel name and we'll confirm delivery. Most halls on and around campus are covered.

How do I pay?

Pay on delivery, by cash or M-Pesa, once your order has arrived and you've checked it.

What if an item is out of stock?

We'll let you know on WhatsApp as soon as we see your order, and suggest the nearest alternative or an updated delivery time.

Can I ask about an item that isn't listed yet, like patapata or a bucket?

Yes, tap "Ask on WhatsApp" on that item's card, or message us directly. We're adding items as we confirm fair pricing for each one.
