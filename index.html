Who we are

Built by students, for students

The small campus stalls that freshers rely on in their first week charge more than they need to for the same padlocks, notebooks and bathroom basics. Nafuu stocks the same essentials and passes on the difference, delivered straight to your hostel.

  • Prices checked against the campus market for every item we list
  • No markup on true essentials like noodles: priced to match, not to profit
  • Orders confirmed by a real person on WhatsApp, not an automated system
  • Delivery straight to your hall or hostel, no trip to the stalls required
  • Zero upfront payment: nothing changes hands until your order is in front of you

Nafuu is an independent, student-run supply service serving the University of Nairobi community. It is not an official University of Nairobi enterprise.

Questions answered

Frequently asked questions

When does ordering open?

Nafuu launches during Freshers' Week in August 2026. Join the waitlist above and we'll notify you the moment ordering opens.

Do I have to pay anything before delivery?

No. We never ask for a deposit or upfront transfer. You confirm your order on WhatsApp, we deliver it to your hall, and you only pay (cash or M-Pesa) once you've checked it's correct.

How is this cheaper than the campus market?

We check our prices against the Clabu campus market rate for every item and price just above cost, rather than adding a full retail markup. Essentials like noodles are priced to match exactly.

Do you deliver to all halls and hostels?

Message us on WhatsApp with your hall or hostel name and we'll confirm delivery. Most halls on and around campus will be covered from launch.

What if an item is out of stock?

We'll let you know on WhatsApp as soon as we see your order, and suggest the nearest alternative or an updated delivery time.
